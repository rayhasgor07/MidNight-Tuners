Our goal at MidNight Tuners is to bring that same passion and precision from the golden era of street racing into a modern digital space — where car enthusiasts can learn, share, and tune together.

Inspiration behind the name "MidNight Tuners"


The legendary street racing club known as the “Mid Night Club” is essential knowledge for any fan of street racing. Operating from 1987 until 1999, the Mid Night Club was one of the most notorious and highly respected street racing clubs. Racing at speeds up to 300kmh (190mph) in Italian exotics such as the Lamborghini Countach or the Ferrari Testarossa, and Japenese cars such as the Nissan 300ZX, Nissan GTRs, the Toyota Supra and the Mazda RX7.
